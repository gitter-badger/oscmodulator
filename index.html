Modulate Anything!

Did you ever wish that button on your foot pedal or drum pad would do more than one thing? Or maybe you wish you could smoothly animate the parameters of your VJ software with the touch of a single button? In that case we've got you covered. OSC Modulator takes incoming MIDI messages and converts them into multiple outgoing OSC messages.
